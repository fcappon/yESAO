Submission Deadline for I3M 2023 Extended until May 15th!

We would like to draw your attention to the upcoming 20th International Multidisciplinary Modeling & Simulation Multiconference (I3M 2023) taking place in Athens from September 18-20, 2023. As part of I3M 2023, there will be a yESAO-endorsed track about Modelling and Simulation of Device Interface with Physiological Environment. Don't miss this opportunity and submit your contributions NOW!

  • Contributions in the form of a paper (minimum length of 3 pages, maximum length of 10 pages): https://lnkd.in/eUC3-Ba7
  • Submission deadline: May 15th, 2023 https://lnkd.in/eXzmrAky
  • Three different awards will be granted for submitted papers: https://lnkd.in/eUC3-Ba7
  • Important: Online participation is possible!
