Coordinator

My dissertation deals with surface functionalisation of cochlear implants using electrospun superhydrophobic drug-releasing surfaces and SLIPS systems to avoid unwanted connective tissue overgrowth. During the yESAO I am looking after our new members in the Buddy Programme!

Core Team

The main aim of my PhD is the design of new 3D liver in vitro models based on hydrogels and hepatic cell culture. In 2022 I attended yESAO and it has been one of my best experiences during my PhD!

Theodor Abart

Vienna, Austria

Core Team

As a member of the CARE Team at the Medical University of Vienna, Department of Cardiac Surgery, I contribute to research on monitoring and control algorithms for implantable blood pumps. Our work focuses on utilizing pump parameters to monitor the hemodynamic status of LVAD patients and, based on this, control the LVAD to achieve favorable cardiac mechano-energetics and physiological regulation. We employ in-silico simulations, in-vitro hybrid mock circulatory loop measurements, and preclinical validation in a novel chronic animal model.
