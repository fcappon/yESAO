FAQs

General:

Q: What are the benefits I get from a yESAO membership?

A: As a yESAO member, you will get connected to a network of like-minded peers in the field of artificial organs. This greatly facilitates knowledge exchange across borders and may help to join forces on specific research questions. Further, with the yESAO membership you are eligible to apply for the yESAO Exchange Award, which will provide financial support for a short research stay abroad. With the yESAO membership, you are furthermore typically eligible for a reduced registration fee for the annual yESAO meeting where the yESAO community meets to discuss the latest scientific findings.

Please visit the section "Membership" on our official website for more information: https://youngesao.com/membership.

Q: What are the formal requirements to apply for the yESAO Exchange Award?

A: The yESAO Exchange Award is open for all yESAO members with an age below 35, who did not already win the award in the past. Applicants need to find a collaborating host research institution for the exchange and submit a short application in compliance with the advertised call for submission. The opening of the next call for submissions will be advertised on Linkedin and on the yESAO Website.

Please visit the section "News" on our official website for more information:
https://youngesao.com/news

Conference-Specific

Q: Registration: do I need to be a member of yESAO to take part at the yESAO Conference?

A: No. But if you are not an ESAO or IFAO member, a fee of 85€ is requested (50€ participation fee + 35€ of Membership) for the participation at the yESAO Conference only. This does not include the yESAO social events. Additional information is available on the ESAO 2025 official website, in the section "Ticketing".

https://www.esao2025.com/

Q: Is there an age barrier to join the yESAO program?

A: Anyone can participate at the yESAO Conference. But yESAO membership is for people below 35 years of age only.

Q: Is there a dress code for the yESAO congress? What do you suggest to pack?

A: There is not strict dress code for the yESAO Meeting. Typically, people will dress smart casual, thus a combination of "business" cloths with a few casual clothes (e.g. blue jeans paired with a formal jacket).

Q: Can I attend the yESAO only?

A: Yes, during the (y)ESAO Conference 2025 this is possible. Please check the ESAO 2025 official website in the section "Ticketing":

https://www.esao2025.com/

Q: Can a member of IFAO join the yESAO Conference?

A: Yes, if you are ESAO or IFAO member, for the participation at the yESAO Conference only a fee of 75€ is requested. This fee, however, does not include the yESAO social events. Additional information is available on the ESAO 2025 official website in the section "Ticketing":

https://www.esao2025.com/

Q: Where can I find information about the social activities during the yESAO Conference?

A: For the yESAO social events further information is available on the yESAO official website:
https://youngesao.com or upon request at our Contact Page.

Q: Who can I contact (and how) if I have organizational/technical questions about yESAO?

A: In case of any queries, you can always reach out to the yESAO Core Team through our Contact Page.

Q: Who can I contact (and how) if I need help/aid in case of an emergency during the yESAO Conference?

A: We created a dedicated Safety Group for the yESAO Conference. The members of the Safety Group are part of the yESAO Core Team and their contacts will be provided during the registration or upon request through our Contact Page.
