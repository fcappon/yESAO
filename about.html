About us

Welcome to the European Society of Artificial Organs! We are a young, dynamic research society comprised of some of the brightest minds in the field of artificial organs. Our aim is to advance the field of artificial organ technology through innovative research and collaboration.

Our members come from all over Europe and bring a diverse range of expertise, including engineering, biology, and medicine. Together, we are dedicated to finding new and improved ways to develop artificial organs that can improve the lives of millions of people around the world.

As a society, we are committed to promoting the exchange of ideas and fostering interdisciplinary collaboration among our members. We host regular events, including conferences and workshops, to bring our members together and provide opportunities for them to share their research and insights.

Whether you are a seasoned researcher or just starting out, we invite you to join us in our mission to make a difference in the field of artificial organs. Together, we can help to create a future where everyone has access to the life-saving technology they need to live their best life.
