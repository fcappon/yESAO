Benefits

Peer group networking

As a member of yESAO, you'll have access to a peer group network of like-minded individuals who are passionate about advancing the field of artificial organs. Connect with research groups from other universities and companies, exchange ideas and experiences, and discuss career development paths with those who share your interests.

yESAO exchange program

Through the yESAO exchange program, you'll have the opportunity to experience research at various European institutes, with financial support and expenses covered by grants. Plus, your research work will receive prominent exposure to the ESAO audience, helping you to build your professional network and gain recognition for your contributions to the field.

yESAO annual meeting

And don't miss the annual yESAO meeting, where you'll be at the forefront of discussions on technical challenges and methodologies related to artificial organs. The meeting features inspiring talks by participants and invited speakers, as well as workshops designed to help shape new ideas and approaches.

Join yESAO today and become a part of a vibrant community of young scientists, engineers, and clinicians who are driving innovation in the field of artificial organs. If you have any questions about the specifics, please contact us at yesao@esao.org

Application and Renewal

With an annual fee of just 30 EUR, yESAO membership is an affordable alternative to regular ESAO membership. To apply, simply visit the application page on the main ESAO website and download the form. In addition to joining yESAO, we also invite you to sign up for our mailing list. This is a great way to stay up-to-date on the latest developments in artificial organs and connect with other members of the yESAO community. Don't miss out on this exciting opportunity to join a community of young scientists, engineers, and clinicians who are making a difference in the field of artificial organs. Apply for yESAO membership today!
